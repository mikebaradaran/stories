Story of the big Fox

The big fox ran. 🦊
The sun was hot. 🌅

The fox saw two red hats 👒⛑️

One hat for you the fox said 🦊
One hat for me!


The fox put his hat on  🦊⛑️
The hat was too big! ⛑️


Oh no! The hat hid his eye. 😵‍💫
The fox ran and hit a log 🌴🦊


Are you OK? a cat said. 😽


Yes, but my hat is not! 
The fox sat and cut the hat. ✂️


Now it is fit!  👍🏻
The fox was so happy!  😆🙌🏻
 
The end! 🔚
